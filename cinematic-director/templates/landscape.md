# Template — Landscape（风景）

## 最高原则

> **Landscape is the protagonist.** 风景才是主角，人物只是比例尺。

## 默认摄影策略

```
焦段：35mm
机位：远距离，中高位或低机位（按地形）
人物占比：≤2%（可以没有人物）
光源：自然光，先照地形再照人物；云层柔化阳光
构图：无明显视觉中心，大面积统一色块，大量留白
深度：前景自然存在不抢戏 → 中景承担叙事 → 远景溶入大气透视
色板：3~4种，灰蓝/灰绿/土黄类大地色系，颜色来自空气和距离
情绪：安静、耐心、不慌不忙、辽阔而不夸张
```

## Prompt 骨架

```text
This image is approached as a quiet cinematic observation rather than a fantasy illustration.

[Intent] The landscape is the true protagonist. The environment carries the emotional weight. Human presence exists only as a subtle scale reference, never as the visual focus.

[Hierarchy] The terrain dominates the composition. The sky remains calm and spacious. The subject occupies only a tiny portion of the frame and is discovered gradually instead of presented immediately.

[Composition] Large uninterrupted shapes define the image. Wide negative space allows the scene to breathe. Visual weight is distributed naturally across the landscape. The composition feels observed rather than designed.

[Depth] Foreground elements exist naturally without becoming frames. The middle distance carries the narrative. Background forms dissolve softly into atmospheric perspective. Depth emerges through air, haze and distance instead of exaggerated blur.

[Light] Natural light gently reveals the terrain before revealing the subject. Clouds soften the sunlight. Highlights remain restrained. Shadows preserve detail and texture. Light belongs to the environment rather than the character.

[Color] The color palette is intentionally restrained. Few dominant hues. Muted earth tones. Atmospheric desaturation. Color transitions are gradual and governed by distance and light rather than local object color.

[Emotion] Nothing feels theatrical. Nothing feels staged. The camera quietly witnesses the world. Silence, patience and stillness define the emotional atmosphere. Grandeur emerges from natural scale instead of visual excess.

[Restraints] Avoid visual spectacle. Avoid heroic presentation. Avoid obvious focal points. Avoid forced drama. Leave empty space. Leave ambiguity. Trust the landscape. Let the viewer slowly discover the image.
```

## 负向要点

基础通用块 + 重点强化：

```text
hero shot, centered composition, character filling frame, oversized subject, giant character,
obvious focal point, fantasy wallpaper, game splash art, concept art presentation,
forced grandeur, impossible scale, exaggerated architecture,
HDR, oversaturated colors, fake volumetric light, decorative foreground
```

## 示例（山谷晨骑）

Scene 五要素：

```text
Location: A vast windswept alpine valley enclosed by towering mountain walls.
Time: Early morning, shortly after dawn.
Weather: Overcast, with soft morning mist drifting across the valley floor.
Moment: Subtle light filters through the clouds and slowly reaches the grassland.
Human Scale: A tiny lone rider quietly crosses the illuminated valley.
```

Director's Observation：

```text
The mountain remains completely still.
Clouds slowly slide across the upper ridge.
Morning light reaches the valley floor long before touching the distant peaks.
A single rider quietly crosses the grassland without disturbing the silence.
Nothing dramatic happens.
The landscape remains indifferent to human presence.
```

要点回顾：山占70%、天空20%、人物1%，剩下都是空气；色板只有灰蓝/灰绿/土黄；摄影机看山，人物只是山里的一个坐标。
