# Template — Xianxia（修仙）

## 最高原则

> **尺度感（Scale），不是特效感（Effects）。**
> 仙气来自巨大的自然尺度与人的渺小，不来自灵气粒子和神光。

修仙题材是"游戏海报风"的重灾区：AI 默认收敛到手游启动页——人物巨大、灵气环绕、金光紫光、悬浮法阵。本模板的全部工作就是对抗这种收敛。

## 核心决策

1. **今天拍的是山/云海，修仙者只是比例尺**（Landscape First 的极端形态）
2. **神性来自克制**：`Immense without exaggeration` ——巨大但不夸张
3. **光必须有来源**：月光/晨光/云层透光，不是"发光的人"
4. **发光元素若必须存在，压到最低**：`a faint glow`，且永远不是画面最亮的大面积区域

## 默认摄影策略

```
焦段：35mm
机位：低机位（衬托尺度），距离远
人物占比：~10% 以下，常为剪影或极小的白点
光源：云层透光/月光/黎明前的天光；光先照云海和山，再照人物
构图：云海/山体占绝对主导，垂直尺度感，大量负空间
色板：蓝白/灰蓝/墨青/淡金，3~4种；颜色来自大气
情绪：敬畏、孤高、亘古、Timeless scale / epic silence（作为情绪而非标签）
```

## Prompt 骨架

```text
This image is approached as a quiet cinematic observation of an immense immortal landscape rather than a fantasy game illustration.

[Intent] The mountains and the sea of clouds are the true protagonists. The cultivator exists only as a scale reference against the enormity of the world. The image is about awe and solitude, not power.

[Hierarchy] The cloud sea and towering peaks dominate the composition. The sky stays soft and vast. The cultivator occupies roughly ten percent of the frame or less, discovered gradually within the landscape.

[Moment] Dawn has not yet arrived. The first pale light slowly spreads across the upper surface of the cloud sea. The world is changing so slowly that time itself feels suspended.

[Photography Strategy] A natural 35mm cinematic perspective from a low distant position. The camera observes the scale of the world rather than the figure. Layered clouds and ridgelines create immense vertical depth.

[Light] Light belongs to the sky and the clouds before touching the figure. Highlights remain restrained. The figure carries at most a faint, believable glow, never brighter than the sky.

[Color] A restrained palette of pale blue, cool white, ink-toned mountain silhouettes and a trace of early gold. Colors transition through atmosphere rather than saturation.

[Emotion] Ancient and solemn atmosphere. Timeless scale. Stillness becomes the emotion. Immense without exaggeration.

[Restraints] Avoid spectacle. Avoid glowing effects. Avoid heroic framing. Trust the scale of nature. Leave silence. Leave emptiness. Let the viewer slowly discover the cultivator.

[Scene 五要素 + Director's Observation]
```

## 负向要点

基础通用块 + 修仙专用块（最重要的部分）：

```text
overpowered aura, excessive energy effects, too many particles, explosion everywhere,
floating magic circles, over glowing weapon, super bright spiritual energy, flashy effects,
rainbow colors, neon lighting, fantasy wallpaper, mobile game splash art,
MMORPG loading screen, poster art, key visual, cover illustration,
character occupying entire frame, hero pose, dynamic action pose,
flying toward camera, camera facing character directly, perfect centered framing,
glowing edges, bloom overload, fake god rays, fake volumetric light
```

## 示例（云海山巅 · 剑仙原型）

Scene 五要素：

```text
Location: A solitary mountain peak rising above an endless sea of clouds.
Time: The last hour before dawn.
Weather: Slow-moving cloud layers, thin cold mist around the summit.
Moment: The first pale light begins to spread across the surface of the cloud sea.
Human Scale: A lone sword cultivator stands at the edge of the summit, barely visible against the sky.
```

Director's Observation：

```text
The cloud sea moves without sound.
The mountain has stood here long before anyone learned to fly.
The first light touches the clouds, not the figure.
He watches the horizon and does not move.
Nothing needs to happen.
The world remains indifferent to those who seek immortality.
```
