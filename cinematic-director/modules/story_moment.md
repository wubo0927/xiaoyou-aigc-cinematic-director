# Module 3 — Story Moment（故事瞬间）

## 职责

回答：**这一秒，刚刚发生了什么？**

摄影拍的是 Moment，不是状态。不要写"她拿着剑"，要写"她刚刚停下脚步"。

## 决策规则

### 1. 找"刚刚"——时间上的前一秒或后一秒

| 状态（禁止） | 瞬间（要求） |
|---|---|
| 女孩站着 | 女孩刚刚回头 |
| 女孩在海边 | 女孩正吹着海风，刚把头发别到耳后 |
| 女侠拿剑 | 她刚刚停下脚步，举剑至胸前 |
| 两人对峙 | 两根竹子缓慢向彼此回弹，下一秒必然相撞 |

### 2. Moment Before 优先——最有张力的一帧，不是最激烈的一帧

> Anticipation is stronger than Action.

箭还没射出、剑还没相撞、人物还没转身、暴雨还没落下。观众的大脑会自动补全下一秒。

### 3. "什么都没发生"本身就是强瞬间

```text
She stops. The wind passes. Nothing happens.
Nothing moves except the bamboo leaves.
Time appears suspended.
```

### 4. 瞬间要给出内在动机的暗示，但不解释

她为什么看剑？——不说破，只写"She seems to be remembering something."（留给观众补故事）

## 英文句式库

```text
She has just stopped walking.
She raises the sword horizontally before her.
She is not inspecting the weapon. She seems to be remembering something.
Nothing moves except the bamboo leaves.
The sword catches a faint strip of moonlight.
Time appears suspended.

Nothing has happened yet. Everything is about to happen.
The bent bamboo slowly rebounds toward one another.
The entire forest appears to hold its breath.

Soft daylight slowly moves across her face.
She briefly looks away before returning her gaze.
Her breathing feels calm. Silence becomes part of the portrait.
```

## 生活化瞬间库（人像/城市题材）

```text
女孩刚刚回头。
女孩正在等地铁。
女孩看见远方。
女孩吹着海风。
女孩走过雨后的街道。
晨光刚刚照到她的侧脸。
她沉默了一秒。
```

对应英文：

```text
She has just turned her head.
She is waiting for the train, unaware of being seen.
The morning light has just reached the side of her face.
She walks through the street after rain, lost in thought.
A second of silence passes over her.
```

## 与场景（Scene）的分工

- Story Moment 是**人物层面**的瞬间（她刚做了什么/正在经历什么）
- Scene 的 Moment 要素是**世界层面**的瞬间（第一缕光刚到谷底、云正翻过山脊）
- 两者要在同一时间点上咬合：`The first sunlight reaches the valley floor` + `A lone traveler has just stopped to watch it`
