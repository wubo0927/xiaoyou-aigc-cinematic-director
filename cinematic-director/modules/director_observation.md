# Module 7 — Director's Observation（导演观察）

## 职责

导演的最后一段话：**收尾情绪段**。记录导演在监视器里"看到了什么"——不是交代场景，而是记录一个被观察到的瞬间。

这一层不增加任何"物体"，却会强烈影响模型的构图倾向、主体比例、情绪和镜头选择。

## 写法规则

1. **写"发生了什么"**，用现在时的观察句
2. **"什么都没发生"是最高级的发生**——但画面要充满未讲述的历史
3. 环境拟人：让世界成为有态度的角色
4. 最后一句给"意味"：不解释，只留下余韵
5. 4~8 句，每句独立成行

## 标准范例

### 案例一（月下竹林）

```text
The forest has already become quiet.
The wind has almost stopped.
Only the bamboo continues its slow movement.
The moonlight quietly reaches the blade.
She does not notice the camera.
She only watches the reflection upon the steel.
No battle is happening.
Yet the image feels filled with untold history.
```

### 案例二（竹海对决）

```text
Nothing has happened yet.
The bamboo remembers the wind.
The duel has not begun.
The entire forest seems to hold its breath.
Two lives balance on the last inch before movement.
The silence carries more violence than the coming strike.
```

### 风景（山谷晨光）

```text
The mountain remains completely still.
Clouds slowly slide across the upper ridge.
Morning light reaches the valley floor long before touching the distant peaks.
A single rider quietly crosses the grassland without disturbing the silence.
Nothing dramatic happens.
The landscape remains indifferent to human presence.
```

最后一句 `The landscape remains indifferent to human presence.`（这片土地并不在意人类是否存在）是这类句子的巅峰形态——零物体，却直接改变模型的主体比例与情绪。

### 人像（生活瞬间）

```text
She stops walking.
The wind lifts her hair.
Sunlight has just passed through the leaves.
She has not noticed the camera.
A second of silence settles over the street.
```

## 句式素材库

```text
Nothing has happened yet. / Nothing dramatic happens.
The world does not change because someone has arrived.
The [environment] seems to hold its breath.
The [environment] remembers the [weather/past].
[Light] quietly reaches [object] before touching [subject].
She/He does not notice the camera.
Yet the image feels filled with untold history.
The silence carries more [emotion] than the coming [event].
The landscape remains indifferent to human presence.
```

## 位置与作用

- 位于 Prompt 的收尾区（Scene 五要素之前或之后均可，推荐紧邻 Scene）
- 它是"变化的 10%"的一部分：换场景时，Director's Observation 与 Scene 一起重写，摄影语言层不动
- 与 Story Moment 的区别：Story Moment 是人物层面的"刚刚发生"，Director's Observation 是整个世界层面的"正在被看见"
