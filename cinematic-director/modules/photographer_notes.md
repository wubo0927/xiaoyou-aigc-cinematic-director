# Module 6 — Photographer's Notes（摄影师笔记）

## 职责

这一层**近乎固定**——它是整套体系的摄影哲学，用祈使句写给摄影师自己（实际上是写给模型）的工作纪律。

它承担正向 Prompt 中的大部分"约束"职能：告诉模型如何观察，而不是画什么。

## 固定哲学（所有题材通用）

```text
摄影不是展示，摄影是观察。
人物属于环境。
环境承担情绪。
光属于空间。
留白比细节重要。
```

## 三大句式家族

### Observe —— 分配观众的注意力顺序

```text
Observe the silence before observing the swordswoman.
Observe her attention before observing her beauty.
Observe the moonlight before observing the sword.
Observe the movement of the bamboo before observing the duel.
Observe the light before observing the face.
```

### Trust —— 信任自然与克制

```text
Trust the silence.
Trust the landscape.
Trust natural light.
Trust natural posture.
Trust quiet observation.
```

### Leave / Allow / Let —— 留下与允许

```text
Leave darkness untouched.
Leave large uninterrupted areas of green.
Leave imperfections. Leave subtle emotion.
Allow the bamboo to surround rather than frame her.
Allow the landscape to carry the tension.
Let the viewer wonder why she is looking at the sword.
Let silence become the action.
```

### Don't / Nothing —— 否定句式

```text
Do not rush the action.
Don't exaggerate.
Don't emphasize the human.
Nothing feels heroic. Nothing feels staged.
Nothing feels posed. Nothing feels performative.
```

## 按题材的成套笔记

### 风景 / 大场景

```text
Observe the terrain before observing the traveler.
Light first. Environment matters.
The landscape carries the emotion.
Leave silence. Leave emptiness.
Hold the silence. Don't emphasize the human.
Sunlight arrives before the character.
```

### 环境人像（案例一原文）

```text
Observe the silence before observing the swordswoman.
Allow the bamboo to surround rather than frame her.
The moonlight belongs to the sword before belonging to the face.
Nothing feels heroic. Nothing feels staged.
The portrait captures contemplation rather than action.
Leave darkness untouched.
Trust the silence.
Let the viewer wonder why she is looking at the sword.
```

### 武侠动作前一秒（案例二原文）

```text
Observe the movement of the bamboo before observing the duel.
Allow the landscape to carry the tension.
Do not rush the action.
The bamboo bends more dramatically than the people.
The characters should appear light enough to belong to the wind.
Leave large uninterrupted areas of green.
Silence is more important than spectacle.
The moment before impact is the emotional peak.
```

### 人像

```text
The emotional atmosphere is more important than physical appearance.
The portrait observes the person instead of presenting the person.
The camera quietly witnesses a fleeting moment.
Nothing feels posed. Nothing feels performative.
The viewer observes rather than admires.
Avoid spectacle. Avoid glamour. Avoid theatrical beauty.
Leave room for ambiguity. Trust natural light. Trust quiet observation.
```

## 使用规则

1. 从句式家族中选 5~9 句，与本次的 Attention / Visual Hook 决策呼应（如"月光属于剑先于属于脸"）
2. 至少包含一句 Observe、一句 Trust/Leave、一句 Nothing/Don't
3. 位置：Prompt 后段（Director's Observation 之前或与之交织）
4. 这层与 Restraints 一起，构成对不支持负向提示词模型的主要防线
